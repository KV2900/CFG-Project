Teaching

Becca currently teaches in the English and Relateed Literature department at the University of York. She has extensive teaching experience, teaching undergraduate modules on the medieval literature, and teaching Masters students Old Norse.

Becca has extesnive experience teaching in museum settings, particularly working with the Hull Maritime Museum to led workshops for school children on writing poetry and exploring museum artifacts related to the sea.

Research

Becca's research focuses on the relationships between people and the environments we live in.

Becca is currently editing her PhD thesis for publication as both articles and a monograph.
